SWPedia - Home page

Welcome to the SWPedia home page from Signabroam. Here you will find some websites about the world of the radio and a full wiki about radios stations!

1. Priyom.org

Priyom.org is a full website about the shortwaves radio and number stations.

  1. Click here to access Priyom.org.

2. Eibi Database

The Eibi database is a site that can give you all the list of "stored" known frequencies and more.

  1. Click here to access Eibi.

3. Shortwave.Live

The Shortwave.Live website is used to give you in real time the broadcast time of all shortwave radio stations.

  1. Click here to access Shortwave.Live.

4. SigidWiki ("Signal Identification Guide")

This wiki is intended to help identify radio signals through example sounds and waterfall images. Most signals are received and recorded using a software defined radio such as the RTL-SDR, Airspy, SDRPlay, HackRF, BladeRF, Funcube Dongle, USRP and other devices.

  1. Click here to access SigidWiki.

4. WebArchive

WebArchive is full wayback machine for datas and Websites, the WebArchive also content Radios stuff.

  1. Click here to access WebArchive.
